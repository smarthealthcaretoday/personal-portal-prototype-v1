What is ElaGem

  • ElaGem for the personal use

  • ElaGem for the personal use – simulation tool and software application that provides the user with the rights and opportunity to assess personal health condition, make informed decisions about the health, and gives the most effective and quick access to the proper health service.

  • ElaGem for the society

  • ElaGem for the society – this is an assistant for the society to provide citizens with the best health management and protection tools based on the public health indicators, healthcare organisation and available resources
